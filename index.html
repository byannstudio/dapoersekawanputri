About Us

Dapoer Sekawan Putri is a homemade culinary business from Kartasura, Sukoharjo. We produce:

  • Caramel Banana Chips (variants: Chocolate, Vanilla Milk, Sweet, Ginger, Cinnamon, Salty)
  • Frozen Beef Rendang
  • Frozen Steamed Chicken

We are committed to delivering delicious food with a distinctive Indonesian flavor.
